Basics

Season Statistics

Saturday Night Live has been on the air for 41 seasons. Across these seasons, there have been 808 episodes. The season with the highest number of episodes was the first season, with 24 episodes. The 33rd season is the season with the least number of episodes. There were only 12 episodes that season due to a writers strike. Similarly, seasons 6 and 13 had thirteen episode seasons due to writers strikes.
